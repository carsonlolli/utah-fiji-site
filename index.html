Our Pillars

What We Stand For

The five principles that have guided Phi Gamma Delta for more than 175 years.

01

Friendship

The bonds formed within our chapter are built to last a lifetime. We show up for each other through every season.

02

Knowledge

Academic excellence is non-negotiable. We push each other to think critically and reach higher.

03

Service

Giving back is woven into everything we do — from philanthropy events to local volunteer work.

04

Morality

We hold ourselves to the highest standard. Integrity is the foundation of who we are.

05

Excellence

In every area of life we push each other to reach our full potential and settle for nothing less.

Built on these five

Every tradition and standard of this chapter is shaped around them.
